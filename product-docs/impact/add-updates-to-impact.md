# Add updates to cleanup

### Step 1: Select cleanup to add update

Select a cleanup you want to add update to from the "Cleanups" tab on the bottom of the page and click on the "Add Update" button as shown in the image below.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/upd 0 (1).png" alt="" width="375"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

### Step 2: Add update and submit&#x20;

Fill in the updates and upload image/video based on your preference. Then, click on the "post update" button.
