# Start a new cleanup

### Step 1: Click on the "Cleanups" button

On your Dashboard, click on the "Cleanups" button to navigate to its tab.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/cl0.png" alt="" width="375"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

### Step 2: Allow App to know your location

When you get prompted, choose between "allow while visiting the site" and "allow this time" to allow app access your location.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/cl1.png" alt="" width="155"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

### Step 3: See list of added Cleanups

On this page, you'll find a list of added cleanups, you can browse cleanup event near you in map and list view.

<div><figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/cl2 (2).png" alt="" width="375"><figcaption></figcaption></figure> <figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/cl3 (2).png" alt="" width="375"><figcaption></figcaption></figure></div>

### Step 4: KYC Verification required

If you get the KYC verification alert, it means that you haven't completed KYC. KYC verification is required before you can organize or join cleanups.&#x20;

{% hint style="info" %}
See [kyc-verification](../profile/kyc-verification/ "mention")
{% endhint %}

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/kyc req.png" alt="" width="375"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

### Step 5: Fill in Cleanup details

After a successful KYC verification, you will be able to submit the details of your cleanups including title, date, time, location and others as presented in the images below.

<div><figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/org cl1.png" alt="" width="375"><figcaption></figcaption></figure> <figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/org cl2.png" alt="" width="375"><figcaption></figcaption></figure></div>



<div><figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/org cl3.png" alt="" width="375"><figcaption></figcaption></figure> <figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/org cl4.png" alt="" width="375"><figcaption></figcaption></figure></div>

### Step 6: Sign Transaction

Click on the "Sign and Continue" button.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/sign (2).png" alt="" width="199"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

After your cleanup has been created, it will be displayed on the "created" tab and upon Admin's approval it would become published and visible to other eco-champions around you.&#x20;

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/cl created.png" alt="" width="375"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

{% hint style="info" %}
Interested in joining an already created cleanup? Read [request-to-join-a-cleanup.md](request-to-join-a-cleanup.md "mention")
{% endhint %}
